Privacy Policy for Squad explode

Effective date: June 8, 2026

Squad explode is provided by Squad Explode Studio. This policy explains how the Android and iOS game handles information.

Information handled by the game

The game may save progress, settings, currency balances, level progress, audio preferences, and ad-related preferences on your device using local app storage.

Advertising

The game uses Google Mobile Ads to show ads. Google Mobile Ads may process device identifiers, approximate location derived from network information, ad interaction data, diagnostics, and other information as described by Google's advertising and privacy documentation. Consent messages may be shown where required.

Analytics

Unity Analytics is not enabled in the current build because Unity Cloud project and environment details were not supplied.

GameAnalytics

The Android build uses GameAnalytics for non-personal gameplay events, such as app start, session, scene, and level progression events. The current iOS build does not initialize GameAnalytics unless separate iOS analytics credentials are configured later. The current integration does not send names, email addresses, precise location, contacts, photos, microphone input, or other sensitive personal content in custom analytics events.

No account or cloud sync

Squad explode does not require account registration. The current build does not upload game progress to a developer server and does not provide cloud sync.

Purchases

Real-money in-app purchases are not enabled in the current build. Paid/no-ads purchase controls are hidden. Free and rewarded-ad reward surfaces remain available.

Data sharing

Local game progress is stored on your device. Advertising data may be processed by Google Mobile Ads and its partners to provide, measure, and improve ads according to your consent choices and applicable law. GameAnalytics may process non-personal gameplay analytics events for app quality and balancing.

Data retention and deletion

Local game records remain on your device until you delete app data or uninstall the game. Ad-related data is handled by Google Mobile Ads according to Google's policies and your device/account settings. GameAnalytics event retention is handled by GameAnalytics according to its service terms.

Children

The game is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 unless the publisher explicitly configures it for a child-directed release and updates ads, consent, content rating, and store settings accordingly.

Security

Because local progress is stored on your device, protect your device with the security features available on Android or iOS.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact Squad Explode Studio at squadexplode@gmail.com.
